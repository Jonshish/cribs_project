ABOUT

CRIBS IS ALL ABOUT YOU

It began with just two of us. We weren’t happy with the real estate brokers we were working for. Each one promised a grand environment but under-delivered. They lacked trustworthiness, care for the customer, and didn’t provide their agents with the right resources.

Something that also bothered us greatly were countless horror stories about rental scams. We felt passionately, that renters need to be educated and they need an option that is catered to their home search. Something that’s user friendly, forward thinking and honest!

We began brainstorming and trying to peer into the future. “There needs to be a tech element” we both proclaimed! That’s where a third person joined us on the CRIBS mission. With their knowledge of coding, real estate expertise and amazing ideas, the excitement became overwhelming.

CRIBS NYC was officially born. We are going to help a lot of people find their perfect roommate and their perfect apartment as easily and efficiently!

It has been quite a fun journey but we’re just getting started! At CRIBS, we look forward to helping you find your dream apartment at the best price possible and with ease. Feel free to reach out to us directly with any questions, comments or concerns you may have!
